About NTU HPC

NTU HPC is the only student supercomputing team in Singapore. We are a group of talented students who are passionate about high performance computing.

We particiate in major student cluster competitions like ASC, ISC, and SC.

Besides that, we also have weekly training sessions in NTU to teach NTU students about high performance computing
