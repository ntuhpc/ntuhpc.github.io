About NTU HPC

The Nanyang Technological University High Performace Computing Team (NTU HPC) is the only student cluster competition team in Singapore. We particiate in major student cluster competitions including ASC, ISC, and SC.

In our team, you can find Linux power users, sysadmins, performance analysts, code optimizers, hardware specialists, and machine learning practitioners.

We host regular meetings every week to discuss various HPC related topics (e.g. Linux, parallel programming, and performance optimization) and prepare for the competitions.

NTU HPC is proudly sponsored by National Supercomputing Centre (NSCC) Singapore, Nanyang Technological University, JOS, NVIDIA, Dell EMC, and Mellanox Technologies.

History

Won Highest LINPACK Award (breaking world record again at 56.51 TFLOPS/s) and ranked 2nd overall.
ISC18
Won 2nd Place Overall.
SC17

ISC17
Won Deep Learning Excellence Award and ranked 4th overall.
SC16
First appearance in SC. Ranked 1st in secret application (GROMACS) and 5th overall.
ISC16
First appearance in ISC. Ranked 3rd in HPL benchmarking.
ASC16
Won Application Innovation Award (HPCG) and Fan Favorite Team (Twitter).
ASC15
Won Highest LINPACK Award (11.92 TFLOPS/s) breaking the world record.
ASC14
First appearance in ASC. Won Silver Prize (ranked 2nd overall).
